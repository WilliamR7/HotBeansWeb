Hot Beans Web

Job Requirements

Senior developer

What are the requirements

Location: UK (Remote/Hybrid)

Key Requirements:

Proven Experience: 5+ years in web development (full-stack preferred), with a strong portfolio of completed projects.

Technical Expertise: Proficiency in JavaScript (ES6+), TypeScript, Node.js, and frameworks like React, Vue.js, or Angular. Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases, API design (RESTful/GraphQL), and version control (Git).

Back-End & DevOps: Experience with Express.js, Docker, cloud platforms (AWS/GCP), and CI/CD processes.

Leadership Skills: Experience mentoring teams, conducting code reviews, and working in Agile environments.

Problem-Solving & Architecture: Strong ability to design scalable, high-performance solutions and solve complex technical challenges.

Communication: Clear and effective communication with technical and non-technical teams.

Best Practices: Solid understanding of clean code, design patterns, and security best practices.

Qualifications:

Degree in Computer Science or equivalent, or relevant professional experience.

UK work eligibility.

Benefits:

Competitive salary, flexible work hours, and professional development opportunities.



What it entails

As a Senior Developer at Hot Beans Web, you’ll be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-performance web applications. You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams to deliver seamless user experiences and scalable solutions. Your role will involve mentoring junior developers, conducting code reviews, and ensuring best practices in coding, testing, and deployment. You will also have the opportunity to lead technical decisions, improve system architecture, and drive innovation within the team.

Expect to work in a dynamic environment, collaborating with both technical and non-technical stakeholders while staying ahead of industry trends and technologies.
